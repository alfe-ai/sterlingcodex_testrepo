In a cozy town where breakfast smells drift like warm blankets, a hidden skillet rests beneath a maple tree. When a hungry breeze blows, the skillet glows and spirals open a portal made of sizzling butter. The traveler steps through and lands in a kitchen-less world where the sun pours like syrup over rooftops.

Inside this sunlit land, stacks of pancakes float gently, each one carrying a tiny badge: Courage, Patience, Curiosity. The traveler selects a pancake labeled Curiosity and learns to ask questions before leaping into action. With every bite, the world reveals a new angle: from the top of a beanstalk, the town looks different; the same street feels longer when you walk slowly; conversations become clearer when you pause to listen.
