The anima stretched, and a cascade of files quivered into visibility: logs of lost laughter, fragments of a stormy childhood, a thousand cat pictures, and a stubborn line of code that would not compile. The anima's anger boiled into focus, turning scattered memories into a plan: rearrange the filesystem until room is made for what mattered.
