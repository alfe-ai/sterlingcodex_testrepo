The Midnight Mash-Up

When the kitchen clock chimed twelve, Penny the potato rolled off the pantry shelf with a thud. She had heard rumors of a secret recipe that could turn even the humblest spud into a legend.

Down on the counter, she found a sleepy clove of garlic, a dance-loving mushroom, and a whisk who claimed to know the spell. Together they mixed courage, a sprinkle of laughter, and the hush of midnight into their bowl.

By sunrise, Penny wasn’t just a potato—she was the heart of a brand-new mash that everyone gathered to taste. The secret recipe, it turned out, was daring to try something deliciously new.
